# StudyScout — Design Definition (V1)

## Design Philosophy
StudyScout follows a **dark, minimal, brainy AI aesthetic** designed to encourage focus and typing.
The interface should feel intelligent, calm, and trustworthy — never flashy or playful.

Core feeling:
- Quietly intelligent
- Academically credible
- Modern AI tool

---

## 1. Theme & Background

### Base Theme
- Dark theme only
- Base background color:
  - Near-black or deep navy
- No light mode in V1

### Background Accents
- Subtle spherical gradients (orbs)
- Colors:
  - Dark blue
  - Cyan / electric blue
- Properties:
  - Low opacity
  - Soft blur
  - Used sparingly (1–2 orbs max)
- Purpose:
  - Add depth
  - Avoid distraction

Background gradients should **never overpower content**.

---

## 2. Brand Color System

### Brand Colors (Strict)
- Primary brand colors:
  - Dark blue
  - Cyan / electric blue
- Gradient:
  - Dark blue → cyan

### Usage Rules
- Gradients may be used ONLY for:
  - Logo text
  - Primary button
  - Chatbox glass/glow accents
- Gradients must be subtle and controlled

### Explicit Restrictions
- No blue shades for body text
- No colorful text beyond brand elements
- No additional accent colors (red, green, yellow, etc.)

---

## 3. Typography System

### Text Color Rules
- Primary headings:
  - Pure white
- Body text:
  - Light gray
- Subtext / helper text:
  - Softer light gray
- No colored text anywhere

### Typography Personality
- Hybrid style:
  - Neutral academic
  - Slightly modern / AI feel
- Clean, readable, non-experimental
- Avoid:
  - Decorative fonts
  - Sci‑fi styles
  - Playful typography

---

## 4. Logo Design

### Logo Style
- Text-only logo:
  - Text: "StudyScout"
- Gradient fill:
  - Dark blue → cyan
- No icon, symbol, or illustration

### Placement
- Top-left in navbar
- Modest size
- Not dominant

---

## 5. Layout & Spacing

### Content Density
- Balanced spacing
- Clean but not empty
- Comfortable reading width

### Alignment
- Overall layout centered
- Text mostly center-aligned in hero
- Results content left-aligned within cards

---

## 6. Chatbox (Core Interaction)

### Visual Style
- Glassmorphism-inspired design
- Semi-transparent dark surface
- Soft blur behind chatbox
- Rounded corners

### Focus State
- Subtle blue–cyan glow on focus
- Glow should feel calm, not neon
- Encourages typing without distraction

### Placeholder Text
- Light gray
- Clear and instructional

---

## 7. Primary Button (“Find videos”)

### Default State
- Solid or subtle gradient background
- Brand gradient allowed
- Clean edges

### Hover Interaction
- Combination of:
  - Soft glow (blue–cyan)
  - Micro-lift or scale (very subtle)
- Smooth easing
- No aggressive animation

---

## 8. Results Cards

### Style
- Minimal cards
- Dark background
- Slight separation from page background

### Content Hierarchy
- Video title: white
- Metrics: light gray
- Thumbnail is the main visual element

No gradients inside result cards.

---

## 9. No-Result State

### Message
- Text color: light gray
- Calm, neutral tone

### Action Button
- "Search again"
- Uses same primary button styling

---

## 10. Design Constraints (V1)

- No illustrations
- No emojis
- No excessive animation
- No bright or playful visuals
- No visual clutter

---

## Design Summary
StudyScout should feel like:
- A serious study tool
- Enhanced by AI
- Designed for focus, not excitement

Minimal. Brainy. Trustworthy.
